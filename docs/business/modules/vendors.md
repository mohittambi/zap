# Vendors

**Audience:** Procurement, Vendor Relations, Finance, Operations  
**Plain-language guide to:** Managing supplier and manufacturer records in Zap

---

## What is the Vendor module?

The **Vendor module** is Zap's directory of all suppliers, manufacturers, and trade partners. Every company that sells goods to your business — whether a large manufacturer or a small local supplier — has a record here.

Think of it as a **business address book**, but with full purchase history, linked product SKUs, and contact details, all in one place and accessible by any authorised team member.

---

## What information is stored per vendor?

| Field | What it contains |
|-------|-----------------|
| **Company name** | The vendor's trading name |
| **Contact person** | Primary point of contact |
| **Phone and email** | Communication details |
| **Address** | Billing and shipping address |
| **GST / Tax ID** | For invoicing and compliance |
| **Linked SKUs** | Which products this vendor supplies |
| **PO history** | All inbound purchase orders placed with this vendor |
| **Payment terms** | Net-30, Net-60, etc. |
| **Notes** | Any free-text notes added by the team |

---

## Real-world example: Finding a vendor's details

> **Scenario:** Finance needs the GST number and address of Vendor X to raise a debit note for short-delivered goods.

1. Open the Vendors module in Zap.
2. Search for the vendor by name.
3. The record appears — GST number, full address, and contact details are visible.
4. Finance copies the details directly into the debit note.

No chasing emails. No outdated spreadsheets.

---

## What you can do in the Vendors module

- **Search and browse** vendors by name, category, or region
- **View vendor detail** — full profile including linked SKUs and PO history
- **Add a new vendor** — fill in the form, vendor is immediately searchable
- **Edit existing records** — update contact details, terms, or any other field
- **View linked POs** — see all inbound POs associated with a vendor and their status

---

## How vendors connect to the rest of Zap

```
Vendor record
     ↕
Inbound POs → goods receipt → stock increase
     ↕
Product Listings → vendor-supplied SKUs
     ↕
Finance → debit/credit notes → invoice matching
```

A vendor record in Zap is not just a static profile — it is a live link connecting procurement activity, stock levels, and financial records.
