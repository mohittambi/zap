# Getting Started with Zap

**Audience:** All new Zap users  
**Purpose:** Help you get up and running quickly, regardless of your role

---

## Before you start

To use Zap, you need:
1. A Zap account — your manager or IT administrator creates this for you
2. The correct **role** assigned to your account (see [Roles and Access](roles-and-access.md))
3. A web browser (Chrome or Edge recommended) — no software to install
4. (Optional) The **Zap Ops mobile app** for warehouse and field use

---

## Accessing Zap

| Access type | How to access |
|-------------|--------------|
| Web application | Open your browser and go to the Zap URL provided by your IT team (e.g. `zap.yourcompany.com`) |
| Mobile app (Zap Ops) | Download from the company's internal app distribution or App Store link |
| API access (for integrations) | Request an API key from your system administrator |

---

## Logging in

1. Go to the Zap web URL.
2. Enter your email address and password.
3. You will land on the **home dashboard** — which shows a summary of key activity relevant to your role.

If you have forgotten your password, use the **"Reset Password"** link on the login page.

---

## Finding your way around

The main navigation is on the left sidebar. The items you see depend on your role — not every team member sees every module.

| Sidebar item | What it is |
|-------------|-----------|
| **Inbound** | Vendor POs, GRNs, delivery notes |
| **Outbound** | Channel POs, consignments, labels, reports |
| **Listings** | Product catalogue, SKU search, inventory |
| **Catalogues** | Catalogue builder and exports |
| **Labels** | Label generation tools |
| **Vendors** | Supplier directory |
| **Warehouses** | Storage locations and bins |
| **Forms** | Operational checklists |
| **Focus Lists** | Your saved product shortlists |
| **Bulk Ops** | Mass import and export tools |
| **Settings** | Account settings and preferences |

## Quick-start by role

### I am in Operations / Logistics

**Your most-used areas:**
- **Outbound → Purchase Orders** — check incoming channel orders, acknowledge, update status
- **Outbound → Generate Labels** — print box labels and product labels
- **Inbound → Purchase Orders** — check pending deliveries from vendors
- **Inbound → GRNs** — raise receipts when goods arrive

**Day 1 tip:** Go to Outbound → Purchase Orders and filter by "Status: Open" to see everything waiting for your attention.

---

### I am in Merchandising / Category

**Your most-used areas:**
- **Listings** — search and edit product details
- **Catalogues** — build and export catalogues
- **Focus Lists** — track priority SKUs

**Day 1 tip:** Search for a SKU you know well in the Listings module to see how product data is organised.

---

### I am in the Warehouse

**Your most-used areas:**
- **Inbound → GRNs** — receive goods and record quantities
- **Listings** — look up bin locations
- **Zap Ops mobile app** — scan and check on the go

**Day 1 tip:** Download the Zap Ops mobile app — it makes warehouse tasks much faster than using a desktop browser.

---

### I am in Finance

**Your most-used areas:**
- **Inbound → GRNs** — review received quantities vs invoices
- **Inbound → Debit/Credit Notes** — manage and settle notes
- **Outbound → Reports** — download dispatch reports for reconciliation

**Day 1 tip:** Go to Inbound → GRNs and filter by "Status: Invoiced but not settled" to see what needs your attention first.

---

### I am in Sales / Key Accounts

**Your most-used areas:**
- **Catalogues** — build buyer-ready product catalogues
- **Listings** — check stock levels and product details
- **Outbound** — monitor the status of channel orders for your accounts

**Day 1 tip:** Create a Focus List with the core SKUs for your most important buyer — it saves time every day.

## Common questions

**Q: I can't see the Outbound module — why?**  
A: Your account may not have the outbound role assigned. Contact your administrator.

**Q: I made a mistake in a GRN — can it be undone?**  
A: Corrections can be made by a user with the right permissions. Contact your operations manager.

**Q: Can I use Zap on my phone?**  
A: Yes — the Zap web app works on mobile browsers, and the dedicated Zap Ops app is available for warehouse and field teams.

**Q: How up to date is the stock information?**  
A: Stock levels update in real time as GRNs are raised and dispatches are recorded. The data is always current as long as the team keeps Zap up to date.
