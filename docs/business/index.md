## One-page summary

Zap is the **central operations platform** for managing products, orders, suppliers, and warehouses. It connects to the company's ERP reference system (eAutomate) and brings all key processes under one roof:

- **Inbound** — receive goods, verify invoices, handle returns and debit/credit notes
- **Outbound** — fulfil channel purchase orders, generate consignment paperwork and labels
- **Products** — search, manage, and export the full product catalogue
- **Operations** — run checklists, shortlists, bulk updates, and vendor records

Everything is protected by **role-based access**: each team member only sees and does what their job requires.
