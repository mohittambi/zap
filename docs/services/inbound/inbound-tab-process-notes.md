# Inbound Tab – Process Notes (ZAP)

Operator-facing milestones for inbound work in Zap. **Technical lifecycle** (APIs, statuses, Zap vs GRN-linked debit notes) is in **[inbound-grn-debit-credit-note-flows.md](../../inbound-grn-debit-credit-note-flows.md)**.

---

## I. Vendor Selection

- In the Inbound tab, the first step is to select a **Vendor**.
- Once the vendor is selected, **all items/SKUs** associated with that vendor are displayed.

## II. Item Selection & PO Generation

There are **two ways** to select items:

### a. Using Excel Template

- Download the Excel template from the system.
- Fill in the required items and quantities.
- Upload the completed file back into the system.

### b. Manual Selection

- Items can also be selected manually within the system.

Using either method, a **Purchase Order (PO)** is generated.

#### PO template / catalogue note (gap honesty)

Dedicated “PO Excel template” UX may vary by deployment (vendor vs catalogue workflows). Confirm the screens your team uses in **Inbound → purchase orders / vendor listings**. If upload lives outside this hub, extend this doc locally with your URLs.

---

## III. Goods Receipt (GRN Process)

- When goods are received at the warehouse from the vendor, the GRN Team opens a **GRN** (Goods Receipt Note) in the system.
- The **approved quantity** is entered for all items/SKUs listed in the PO.

### Exception Handling

- If items are **short-received** or **damaged**:
  - **Operator intent:** the system raises a DN (Debit Note) demand tied to receipt issues.

#### Zap today vs automation intent

Operators record shortage/rejected quantities on GRN lines in Zap today. **`closeGrn`** does **not** create a Zap **rate-diff** debit note from shortages alone—that path is **`received_price > audit_price`** ([`generateDebitNote`](../../../src/server/services/grnDebitNoteService.ts)). **Receipt-issue** debit/credit artefacts use **GRN-linked** records and workflows (Pending Debit & Credit hub, uploads, numbering—see [inbound-grn-debit-credit-note-flows.md](../../inbound-grn-debit-credit-note-flows.md) §3). Automated creation of DN **demands** at the moment of quantity entry would be a separate product enhancement if not already configured in your environment.

---

## IV. GRN Closure & Document Upload

- After completing the GRN process, the GRN is **closed**.
- Upon closing, a section appears:

  **GRN Supporting Documents Upload**

- The **vendor's invoice** must be uploaded here.

- Supported formats:
  - JPG
  - JPEG
  - PDF

#### Zap-required order (non-negotiable)

In Zap, **`POST …/close`** **fails** until **at least one** vendor invoice file exists on the GRN ([`closeGrn`](../../../src/server/services/inboundGrnsService.ts)). So in **execution order**:

1. Upload invoice (Documents tab **or** close-flow upload where offered).
2. **Then** confirm **Close GRN**.

Training narratives that describe “close **then** upload” should be reversed for live Zap use. Sections may appear in the UI alongside close; compliance is **invoice-first, then closed**.

---

## V. Invoice Audit Process

- Once the invoice is on file and the GRN is **CLOSED** (see §IV), work moves toward the Invoice Audit Team:
  - Section: **Pending Audit** (`/inbound/pending-audits`)
- The audit team:
  - Verifies invoice data against the receipt.
  - If **rate discrepancy** is found:
    - Capture **`audit_price`** (and quantities) on GRN lines.
    - A **best-effort Zap rate-diff DN** (`DRAFT`) runs on **`POST …/close`** (`tryAutoGenerateDebitNoteAfterGrnClose`). If **`audit_price` is captured only after closure**, use **`POST …/debit-note`** once lines reflect the audited rates so Zap can mint or refresh **`inbound_zap_debit_notes`**. Narrative shorthand “DN on GRN close” matches the **automatic path when discrepant lines exist at close time**.

---

## VI. Physical Invoice Handling (Accounts Team)

- After audit completion:
  - The invoice moves to **Pending Physical Copy Receiving** (Pending Invoice Collection — `/inbound/pending-invoice-collection`).
- The Accounts team:
  - Selects the invoice (row checkbox or bulk),
  - Marks it as **Physical Copy Received** (**`COLLECTED`** via **`PATCH …/grn`**).
- After this action:
  - Zap **allows** retrieval of an Excel workbook (Summary + GRN Items + Debit Note sheets when applicable). Generation happens **when the operator downloads** — **`GET …/invoice-export`** or **Download Invoice Excel** on the GRN Accounts tab (web button gated on **`COLLECTED`**; API does not enforce `COLLECTED`).

---

## VII. DN Handling (If Applicable)

- **Zap rate-diff DN** (`inbound_zap_debit_notes`):
  - If applicable: **Accounts** assigns the real **DN number** (`PATCH …/debit-note` with `dn_number`).

### Final Step

- **Invoice and DN** data can be downloaded in Excel format (**`invoice-export`**).
- After DN is raised, upload **vendor CN copy** (**`POST …/debit-note/cn-copy`**) so the Zap DN demand reaches **`CLOSED`** (alternative administrative **`PATCH`** `{ "close": true }` without file only if allowed by policy—see flows doc §2).

---

## Summary Flow

Vendor Selection → Item Selection → PO Generation → GRN Open → Quantity Update → DN *(if receipt issue)* → GRN Close → Invoice Upload → Audit → DN *(if rate issue)* → Accounts → Physical Copy → DN Assignment → Excel Download

### Zap execution order reminders

- Treat **invoice upload before GRN Close** as mandatory in Zap (§IV).
- Typical queue order: pending **physical invoice collection** before **accounts approval** outcome ([`workflows.md`](workflows.md) pending queues)—your checklist may lump “Accounts” broadly.
- Receipt-issue DN vs rate-diff Zap DN differ (§3 vs §2 in [inbound-grn-debit-credit-note-flows.md](../../inbound-grn-debit-credit-note-flows.md)).
